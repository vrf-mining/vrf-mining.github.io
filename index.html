VRF-based Mining

Simple Non-Outsourceable Cryptocurrency Mining

Runchao HAN (Monash University, CSIRO-Data61)
Haoyu LIN (Bytom Foundation, ZenGo X)
Jiangshan YU (Monash University)

Abstract

This paper introduces VRF-based mining, a simple and effective way of making pooled mining impossible. Instead of using hash functions, VRF-based mining uses Verifiable Random Functions (VRFs) for Proof-of-work (PoW)-based consensus. As VRF binds an output with a secret key, a pool operator should reveal its secret key to outsource the mining process to miners, and miners can anonymously steal cryptocurrency in the pool operatorâ€™s wallet.
